Trashbin: A pastebin like app for non-gui users

Create a new paste:

POST /paste/new HTTP/1.1
{"title": "foo", "text": "bar"}

Consult a paste:

GET /paste/{id} HTTP/1.1

Consult a paste field:

GET /paste/{id}/{field} HTTP/1.1

Delete a paste:

DELETE /paste/{id} HTTP/1.1

That being said, don't give your paste id to anyone, otherwise you risk getting it deleted!
